Business Challenges We Meet

Understand How We Help

  1. Challenge:
    A change to a product’s formulation is necessitated either due to cost saving mandates or ingredient availability. Alternately, a different manufacturer is being considered for a specific product.
    Key questions: Can consumers detect a difference between the current and proposed product?
    Secondary Questions: If a difference is detected, which product is preferred and why?

  2. Challenge:
    A new variant / product / packaging design / format / concept is to be launched, and various options are being considered
    Key Question: Which of the options is preferred?
    Secondary Questions: What are the determinants of the preference? Can one or more of the offerings be improved, and what specific improvements are recommended?

  3. Challenge:
    We believe our product is superior to our key competitor/s and feel a consumer preference claim would aid us in growing our market share?
    Key Question: Do regular users of our product category prefer our product over competitors?
    Secondary Questions: What are the key drivers of preference for the winning product? How can we enhance our product if it is not currently preferred?

  4. Challenge:
    We believe our marketing strategy could be more effective in targetting shoppers to increase sales and market share.
    Key Question: What factors influence the consumer’s decision at the point of purchase for this specific category?

  5. Challenge:
    We believe our marketing tactics could be better timed and targeted to increase frequency of usage.
    Key Question: When, where and how frequently do consumers use our product ?
